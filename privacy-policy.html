This Privacy Policy describes how the "Stop Scrolling Mindlessly" Chrome extension (the "Extension") collects, uses, and handles information. By using this Extension, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect

The Extension is designed to help users reduce mindless scrolling on websites. To achieve this, it may:

  • Monitor your scrolling behavior on web pages (e.g., time spent scrolling, scroll depth).
  • Access the URLs of the websites you visit to determine when to activate features.

Important: All data processing is done locally on your device. No personal data is sent to external servers unless explicitly stated otherwise.

How We Use Information

We use the collected information solely for the following purposes:

  • To provide the core functionality of the Extension, such as displaying reminders or limiting scrolling.
  • To improve the Extension's performance and user experience.

We do not use this information for advertising, marketing, or any other purposes.

Sharing of Information

We do not share, sell, or rent any information collected by the Extension with third parties. Your data remains on your device.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the information processed by the Extension. However, please note that no method of data transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

Data Retention

Data collected by the Extension is stored locally on your device and is automatically deleted when you uninstall the Extension or clear your browser data.

Your Rights

As a user, you have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: You can view data stored by the Extension through your browser's developer tools.
  • Deletion: Uninstall the Extension or clear browser data to remove all stored information.
  • Opt-out: You can disable or remove the Extension at any time.

Children's Privacy

The Extension is not intended for use by children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
